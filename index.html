Synopsis

“EDJOP” is a biopic about Edgar Jopson, a student activist who lived in Martial Law-era Philippines. A young man born to privilege, Edjop woke up to the brutal inequalities in his country and embraced the revolution of the times. He led the First Quarter Storm student rallies, demanding the dictator Marcos Sr. step down, and ultimately joined the underground movement. Edjop, a crucial actor of the Martial Law era, became one of Marcos’ Most Wanted men. He was killed by the military in 1982..
